Company Leave Policy

    Annual Leave (Paid Time Off)

    Full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.

    Leave requests must be submitted through the HRMS portal at least two weeks in advance.

    Approval is subject to manager discretion and team availability.

    A maximum of 5 unused leave days can be carried over to the next year.

    Sick Leave

    Employees are granted 10 days of paid sick leave per year.

    For absences of more than 3 consecutive days, a doctor's note is required.

    Please notify your manager as early as possible on the day of your absence.

    Public Holidays
    The company observes all official public holidays as designated by the local government. These are not deducted from your annual leave balance.
