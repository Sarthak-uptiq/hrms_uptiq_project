Work From Home (WFH) Policy

    Eligibility

    Remote work is available to eligible employees based on their role and performance, subject to manager approval.

    New employees must complete a 90-day probationary period in-office before being eligible for WFH arrangements.

    Expectations

    Employees working from home are expected to be available online during standard business hours (9:00 AM to 5:00 PM).

    A reliable, high-speed internet connection is required.

    The company is not responsible for home office expenses.

    Security

    Employees must ensure that their remote workspace is secure and that company data is protected.

    All work must be conducted on company-issued devices connected via the company VPN.
