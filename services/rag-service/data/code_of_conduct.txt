Company Code of Conduct

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

    Professionalism
    All employees are expected to maintain a professional demeanor in all interactions with colleagues, clients, and partners. Harassment, discrimination, and bullying in any form will not be tolerated.

    Confidentiality
    Employees must protect confidential company information, including client data, financial records, and trade secrets. Unauthorized disclosure of such information is strictly prohibited.

    Conflict of Interest
    Employees must avoid situations where personal interests could conflict with the interests of the company. Any potential conflict of interest must be disclosed to HR immediately.

    Use of Company Assets
    Company assets, including computers and software, are provided for business purposes. Personal use should be minimal and must not interfere with professional responsibilities. All usage is subject to monitoring.
